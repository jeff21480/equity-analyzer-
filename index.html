About Equity Analyzer

Equity Analyzer is an educational valuation tool based on a multi-scenario Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) model.

What this tool does

  • Estimates intrinsic value using Bear / Base / Bull scenarios
  • Works best for companies with positive and relatively predictable cash flows
  • Helps understand sensitivity to growth and discount assumptions

Best suited for

  • Mature companies
  • Blue-chip stocks
  • Established growth companies

Less suitable for

  • Pre-revenue startups
  • Highly cyclical or turnaround businesses
  • Companies with unstable or negative FCF

Important notice

This application is NOT financial advice.
It is for educational and analytical purposes only.
All outputs depend entirely on user assumptions.
